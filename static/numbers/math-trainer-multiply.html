Features of the Math Trainer

Workout Time

You can make good progress by having 3 sessions of 5 minutes every day. But when you just want to practice as you feel choose "1 day".

Cutoff Time

The Cutoff Time is there to help you!

  • With just a few seconds to answer a question it makes you remember, rather than trying to count or use other slow methods
  • At first it seems hard, but with practice you get better and better. And at high speed you get lots of practice.

Choose 4 seconds for best effect.
